About Us

The YAMIFOOD is recognized as the best family restaurant in Belthangady. Restaurant YAMIFOOD Was Started On 1990.The YAMIFOOD restaurant is founded & owned by Anil & Divya.YAMIFOOD restaurant introducing many a forgotten recipes and it is famous for seafood reciepies. We are located on dharamastala and mangalore road. The services provided by them always worked to make the YAMIFOOD a home away from home, combining luxury with the little relaxing touches that we would expect at home.

The must – try dishes at YAMIFOOD restaurant are the Mini Idlis dipped in sambhar, Medu Vada, Dahi Vada, Masala Dosa, Rava Onion Masala Dosa, Mysore Masala Dosa, Rava Kesari. YAMIFOOD believes in delighting the customers with new innovations in its menu and this keeps us way ahead of competition.
